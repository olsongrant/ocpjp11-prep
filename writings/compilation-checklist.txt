
My compilation checklist:
1) Are all checked exceptions handled or declared? E
2) Is there a typing/casting problem?    T
3) Is there an access/visibility problem?  A
4) Is there any unreachable code?     U
5) Are the senders and receivers compatible?   R
6) Are there failures to initialize?    I
7) Are there problems with methods not returning an expected value?      R
8) Are you reading the parentheses correctly??  P
9) Are variables being hidden?  H
10) Does the object involved formally implement the interface necessary (Comparable, AutoCloseable, Serializable, etc.)?
11) Is the variable in scope?
