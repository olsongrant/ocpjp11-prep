Queues and Deques

Deque:

add(E e) -- tail
addAll(Collection) -- tail 
addFirst(E e) -- front
addLast(E e) -- tail
getFirst() -- front
getLast() -- tail
offer(E e)  -- tail
offerFirst(E e) -- front
offerLast(E e) -- tail
iterator() -- head/first to tail/last
pop() -- head/front/first
push(E e) -- head/front/first
remove() -- head/front/first
remove(Object o) -- first/head/front/first
peek() -- head/first
poll() -- first/head/front/first

Queue:
all adds are to the tail
removes are from the head
